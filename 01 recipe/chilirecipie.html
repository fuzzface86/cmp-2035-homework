Chili con Carne

This is recipe is perfect for those cold winter nights, even better as leftovers the next day

25 min. Prep time

1hr 30min. Total time

4 Servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 large pablano peppers
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of Cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of Chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed red peppers

Directions:

  1. Add diced tomatoes, and drained beans to large saucepan over medium heat
  2. Cook beef in pan until beef is brown, add to the saucepan; drain grease into empty aluminum can
  3. Saute the chopped onions, peppers, and garlic with butter for 5 min. Add them to the saucepan
  4. Mix in the cumin, chili powder, oregano, crushed red peppers, salt and pepper
  5. Reduce heat to low; simmer covered for about an hour, stirring occasionally.

Bonus Ingredients and tips:

When plating this dish serve with sour cream and a mix cheese shred.

Make a larger batch for freezer, chili will last for up to one week in the freezer.
